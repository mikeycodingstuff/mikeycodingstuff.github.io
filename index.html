About Me

I'm Mikey, a student on the full stack development course at IO academy.


Since discovering coding I have loved learning more and more about it and applying this in practice to develop my aiblities.

After finishing my Bachelor's and Master's in Philosophy I was unsure what I wanted to do in life; now I am really excited to be starting a career in development.

I have found that a lot of my skills gained through studying philosophy can translate well into a coding environment, such as problem solving or from learning formal logic.
